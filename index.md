I am an Assistant Professor at the [Department of Computer Science and Engineering](https://www.cse.iitb.ac.in/) at [IIT Bombay](https://www.iitb.ac.in/), and a member of [C-MInDS](https://www.cminds.iitb.ac.in/) (Centre for Machine Intelligence and Data Science) at [IIT Bombay](https://www.iitb.ac.in/).

Prior to joining IIT Bombay, I was a postdoctoral researcher at [FAIR Labs (Meta)](https://ai.meta.com/research/) working with [Max Nickel](https://maxn.io) on socially responsible recommendation systems. Before that, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the [Data Science Institute](https://datascience.columbia.edu/) at [Columbia University](https://www.columbia.edu/), hosted by [Prof. Yash Kanoria](http://www.columbia.edu/~yk2577/) and [Prof. Tim Roughgarden](http://timroughgarden.org). I completed my PhD from the [Department of Computer & Information Science](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/) at the [University of Pennsylvania](https://www.upenn.edu/), under the guidance of [Prof. Shivani Agarwal](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Q4ErnU4AAAAJ&hl=en).

## Research Agenda

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, artificial intelligence, and human decision-making. I am broadly interested in understanding and improving how humans interact with ML/AI systems such as recommenders and agentic systems. 

- **Dynamics of Human-AI Interaction** --- feedback loops in recommendations, adaptive preferences, and long-term engagement vs. utility trade-offs. [[ALT'24](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2302.06014.pdf), [FAccT'24](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2406.01611), [WINE'25](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.14013), [ICLR'26](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2509.20304), [NeurIPS'22](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2210.07773.pdf)]

- **Learning from Human Feedback** --- algorithms that learn from pairwise comparisons, choice behavior, and noisy or adversarial responses, including *dueling bandits*, *ranking*, and *peer prediction*. [[NeurIPS'22](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2209.12108.pdf), [ICML'22](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2202.10660.pdf), [TMLR'25](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.12950), [NeurIPS'20](papers/choice_bandits.pdf), [ICML'20](https://proceedings.icml.cc/static/paper_files/icml/2020/4955-Paper.pdf), [ICML'18](papers/ranking-mnl.pdf), [EC'17](papers/agarwal_ec17.pdf)]


- **Responsible AI Design** --- model calibration, creator incentives on platforms, and benchmarking of LLMs and agentic systems. [[AISTATS'26](), [FAccT'24](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2406.01611), [TEAC'20](papers/het-pp.pdf)]

- **Algorithms & ML Theory** --- submodular optimization, stochastic bandits, streaming  and parallel algorithms. [[NeurIPS'24](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2311.13006.pdf), [FOCS'24](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2312.15427.pdf), [SODA'24](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.14950), [COLT'22](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2205.00984.pdf), [SODA'19](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.13351)]

See the full [Research](/research/) page for more details.
